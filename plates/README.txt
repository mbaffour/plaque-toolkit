Batch input folder
===================
Drop your plate photos here -- or drag a folder of photos onto
"Batch Plates (CSV per plate).bat".

Name each photo after its plate so the output CSV matches, e.g.
  WT-1.jpg   T65I-1.jpg   (HEIC / iPhone photos work too).

Results are written to the 'out' folder.

This folder ships EMPTY on purpose: no sample plate images are hosted in
the public repository. Photos you drop here are gitignored, so they are
never committed or pushed.
